Food has a way of bringing people together. And when it’s created by one of Augusta’s most talented culinary teams, dining becomes an entirely different experience, filled with joy, fellowship, and lasting memories. The Partridge Inn offers a variety of dining options for you to explore, from a lively rooftop bar where you can take in the most breathtaking views of Augusta to a comfortable-yet-sophisticated restaurant with an inspired menu of distinctly Southern dishes.

DINING

At 8595, every locally sourced ingredient, dash of spice, and delicate garnish blend seamlessly to put a modern twist on traditional Southern cuisine. The deliciously distinct results stir the soul and harken back to the jubilance and vibrancy of Augusta’s days as our state capital. Experience 8595 – the flavor of Augusta.

THOMAS JACOBS

THOMAS JACOBS

Chef Thomas Jacobs is a perfect example of turning a dream into destiny. As a child growing up in Augusta, he would ride his bicycle by The Partridge Inn and imagine what it would be like to be a chef there. He set his sights on that goal and earned a degree in culinary arts from the Arts Institute of Atlanta. After 15 years of honing his craft as a chef for other restaurants and organizations, Thomas became our executive chef in 2018. That passion and determination are now focused on leading our culinary team and creating the most savory, Southern cuisine in Augusta.
