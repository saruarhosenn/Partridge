HOSPITALITY WITH

History

When you book a stay at The Partridge Inn, you can always expect a unique experience. We are a registered historic hotel in Augusta, Georgia, featuring 143 distinct guest rooms with updated amenities and the perfect combination of historic charm and modern luxury.

As a distinguished member of Hilton’s Curio Collection, we offer an unmatched level of service and quality that is guaranteed to exceed your expectations. Our guest rooms feature plush bedding, high-speed Wi-Fi, functional workspaces, and spa-like bathrooms. Additionally, many of our guest rooms also include private balconies or patios where you can pause and just soak in the experience.

Come experience the Southern hospitality that has made us Augusta’s most beloved hotel for over a century!
