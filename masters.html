Masters Week

A landmark Augusta hotel since 1910, the Partridge Inn is the closest hotel to the famed Augusta National Golf Club. and has a long history with the Masters Golf Tournament. As host to some of the most notable celebrities and dignitaries of its time, “PI” as the locals call it, is “the place to be seen”.

Now, for the first time ever, a select number of rooms and exclusives offerings are available at the Partridge Inn, for those who want to bask in the glow of the Masters this November.

A limited number of guest rooms are available with rates starting at $699 a night. Your stay includes:

  • A welcome champagne toast.
  • A server attended breakfast buffet and omelet station.
  • In room gifts and keepsakes.
  • Nightly turndown service.
  • Enjoy the experience of a timeless Augusta tradition! Book today.

Masters Week Festivities at the Partridge Inn

Special Events & Offerings

The Partridge Inn has an event-filled Masters Week in store for guests and locals alike. Masters’ watch parties will take place throughout the hotel. From exceptional chef-curated dinners to live music nightly, Masters Week will be one to remember. Dining reservations can be made through OpenTable. For rooftop access or for additional questions, please reach out to Madalyn Neal at 762.994.0318 or email mneal@northph.com.

EXCLUSIVE ROOFTOP ACCESS For the first time in 25 years, the Partridge Inn is offering limited, select access to the rooftop bar during Masters Week. On Friday and Saturday evenings, purchase a $100 badge and enjoy the best views in town, two cocktails, light hor oeuvres, and live entertainment. To purchase your rooftop badge, contact Madalyn Neal at mneal@northph.com or call 762.994.0318.

RESTAURANT & BAR to offer all-day bar and dining services. Start your day with a Bloody Mary and end with a chef-curated dinner. Dining reservations through OpenTable.

LIVE MUSIC & WATCH PARTIES Masters by day and live music at night. Enjoy live entertainment Thursday, Friday, and Saturday evening.
