history

Augusta’s Historic Hotel

events

A landmark Augusta hotel since 1910, the Partridge Inn has played host to some of the most notable celebrities and dignitaries of its time. As the closest hotel to the famed Augusta National Golf course, “PI” as the locals call it, is “the place to be seen”.

The Partridge Inn underwent a multi-million dollar renovation to become part of Curio - A Collection by Hilton. We are one of the only hotels in Georgia to be a member of the Historic Hotels of America. Our staff prides itself on carrying the tradition of true “Southern Hospitality”, for more than five generations of travelers. We are proud to say our historic hotel has had more than 1.5 million guests pass through our doors and to continue the tradition today.
