Weddings

The Partridge Inn is the most distinctive hotel in the Augusta area boasting southern charm and a multitude of wedding venues with more than 8,000 square feet of event space. We have hosted generations of Augustans for their special events, including weddings and engagements. Our event spaces make for unique wedding venues, with a variety of backdrops to choose from our rooftop and penthouse area to our cigar bar.

Our rooftop bar offers panoramic views of the city and is a popular venue choice among brides. For receptions, our Champions Ballroom features spacious windows with natural lighting or the Morris Partridge Ballroom, site of the 1923 Presidential Banquet held for Warren G. Harding. Whether you are planning a rehearsal dinner or wedding ceremony, we are here to help make your day entirely yours.
